## January's Meditation Experiment

I conducted a 30-day meditation challenge using the Headspace app, completing 25 of 31 days. The app ramps you up from 10 minutes to 15 to 20 minutes, with guided sessions featuring a narrator with a calming voice.

The benefits I noted included:

- Improved focus on programming tasks
- Reduced distractions from social media
- Better stretching routines
- Enhanced emotional awareness in relationships

## February's Slow Carb Diet Challenge

For my second monthly experiment, I adopted Tim Ferriss's Slow Carb diet from *The 4-Hour Body*. The five core rules are:

1. No white/processed carbohydrates
2. Eat the same meals repeatedly (meat, eggs, vegetables, beans/lentils)
3. No sugar or fruit
4. No caloric beverages
5. One unrestricted cheat day weekly

### Food Preparation

My girlfriend and I spent approximately 4 hours prepping a week's worth of meals, including recipes like:

- **Chicken Tenders** (425 calories)
- **Turkey and Bacon Meatloaf** (423 calories)
- **Blackened Salmon** (279 calories)

I planned to track intake via MyFitnessPal with a 500-calorie daily deficit (1,790 calories plus exercise) and committed to sharing results after one month.
