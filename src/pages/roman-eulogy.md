I adopted Roman from a San Francisco rescue adoption event. Originally planning to adopt a different dog, I fell in love with Roman sitting on his foster mother's lap. Roman arrived underweight with matted fur, suggesting prior abuse. He underwent multiple eye surgeries for cherry eye conditions within his first months.

## Origin of Name & Personality

Roman was named after a character from Grand Theft Auto. He was quirky and loyal, becoming my closest companion during the loneliest and darkest time of my life.

## Relationship

Roman would often stop mid-walk and demand to be carried, leading strangers to offer sympathetic reactions to seeing the chunky dog being transported home. I found these moments endearing rather than frustrating.

## Role in Finding Love

Roman appeared in my dating profile. Jenny, now my wife, claims she "swiped for Roman more than me." Roman and Jenny bonded immediately, and she later adopted Thor, who became Roman's best friend and kenneling companion.

## Adventures

Roman traveled extensively across America and summited Mount Islip in Los Angeles, though he required a 20-minute rest break near the peak. He loved various environments including oceans, rivers, cabins, and our bay window overlooking woods and backyard.

## Personality Quirks

- Could perform "spin" in only one direction
- Would only urinate with his right leg raised
- Licked only one side of his nose
- Had brilliant food-acquisition strategies
- Made humorous facial expressions while panting

## Health Crisis & Wedding

Roman was diagnosed with congestive heart failure in December at age 8, with his left heart chamber measuring 2.5 times normal size. Despite planned ER visits and one surgery, he survived to attend our backyard wedding ceremony, dressed in a tuxedo alongside Thor.

## Final Months

Roman remained unaware of his illness during the eight months following diagnosis. He experienced increased attention, car rides, and special medication treats. We visited the UW Vet Clinic regularly, where staff recognized him as a beloved patient.

## Passing

Roman was euthanized at home in the backyard after his body failed. He fought so hard to stay awake during sedation, fearing separation from his parents.

## Tribute

Saying goodbye broke my heart. But if this pain is the price we pay for all those years of unconditional love, it's a trade I'd make every single time.
